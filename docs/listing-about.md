# USDA Fruit and Vegetable Prices

USDA ERS retail price estimates for 150+ commonly consumed fresh and processed fruits and vegetables. Includes price per pound/pint and normalized price per edible cup equivalent.

## About

The USDA Economic Research Service (ERS) publishes data on agriculture, food, and the environment to inform public and private decision making.

- **191 series** covering produce + form combinations (e.g., Apples Fresh, Apples Juice)
- **Price normalization** via cup-equivalent pricing for cross-form comparisons
- **Annual data points** from 2013 to 2023

## Data Characteristics

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Resolution** | Daily |

| **Sparse Data** | Yes (annual points) |
